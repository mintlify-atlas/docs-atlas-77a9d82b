---
title: "Quickstart: play your first track in Deeztracker"
description: "Connect your Deezer account using an ARL token, browse your library on the dashboard, and play your first track in just a few minutes."
---

This guide walks you through launching Deeztracker for the first time, authenticating with your Deezer account via an ARL token, and playing a track. The whole process takes about five minutes.

<Note>
  Deeztracker authenticates through your personal Deezer ARL session token — not a username and password. You retrieve this token from your browser's cookies after logging in to deezer.com. See [ARL token setup](/configuration/arl-token) for detailed instructions.
</Note>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch Deeztracker">
    Open Deeztracker from your applications menu or desktop shortcut. The app starts and displays the login screen.

    If you have not installed Deeztracker yet, follow the [installation guide](/installation) first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Obtain your ARL token">
    Deeztracker needs your Deezer ARL cookie to authenticate. To get it:

    1. Open [deezer.com](https://www.deezer.com/) in your web browser and log in.
    2. Open **Developer Tools** — press `F12` or `Ctrl+Shift+I`.
    3. Navigate to the **Application** tab (Chrome or Edge) or the **Storage** tab (Firefox).
    4. Under **Cookies**, select `https://www.deezer.com`.
    5. Find the cookie named `arl` and copy its entire value.

    <Tip>
      The ARL value is a long string of letters and numbers. Copy the complete value — partial tokens will not authenticate. For step-by-step screenshots, see [ARL token setup](/configuration/arl-token).
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your ARL token">
    Paste the ARL value into the token field on the Deeztracker login screen, then click **Connect** (or press `Enter`).

    Deeztracker validates the token against Deezer. If the token is valid, you are taken to the dashboard. If authentication fails, double-check that you copied the full `arl` cookie value.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the dashboard">
    The dashboard is your home screen. From here you can:

    - **Search** for tracks, albums, artists, and playlists using the search view.
    - **Browse your library** to see saved playlists and downloaded tracks.
    - **Navigate to an artist or album** by clicking on any result.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play a track">
    To play a track, click the play button next to any song in the dashboard, search results, or a playlist. The player controls appear at the bottom of the window.

    Deeztracker checks whether the track is already saved locally. If it is, playback starts from disk. Otherwise, it streams directly from Deezer.

    <Tip>
      You can control playback from your OS media keys or system tray without switching to the Deeztracker window.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
